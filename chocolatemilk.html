Chocolate Milk Rankings

S Tier

  • Stewarts (Super Rich): ABSOLUTE PERFECT

A+ Tier

  • Byrne Dairy: Very Solid; everything you want in a chocolate milk

A Tier

  • King Dairy: Very Solid, everything you want in a chocolate milk
  • Trumoo: Very Solid, everything you want in a chocolate milk; could use little more chocolate.
  • Colins: Very Solid, everything you want in a chocolate milk; could use little more chocolate. Bonus: Local

A− Tier

  • Nesquik (Bottle): Very Solid, everything you want in a chocolate milk; bottle is subjective.

B+ Tier

  • Promised Land: Very Solid, dark chocolatey (not necessarily my thing) but still very good

B Tier

  • Fairlife: Very good for a lactose-free option.

B− Tier

  • Friendly Farms: Kind of weak, does its purpose as chocolate milk but flavor goes away for a bit.
  • YooHoo: Not advertised as chocolate milk: CHOCOLATE DRINK. Does its purpose as a chocolate milk, perfect after a hard day's work.

F Tier

  • Almond Breeze: Hate Crime to society. Should be called Chocolate Almond Water.
